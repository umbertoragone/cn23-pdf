Filling out a CN23 while standing at a counter, pen in hand, with other people waiting behind you at Poste Italiane's counter is usually the worst time to prepare a customs declaration. It is much easier to do it at home, where you can take a few minutes to review details, descriptions, and codes properly.

With **[CN23.it](/)** you can complete the form directly in your browser, check the PDF preview, and print it before leaving home. That means you can arrive at the post office with the document already prepared instead of writing it by hand in a rush at the Poste Italiane counter.

## Why filling it out at home is easier

The main advantage is simple: you have time to stop and review the form. That matters even more when you need to enter multiple items, declared values, total weight, or HS tariff numbers.

A few extra minutes at home let you:

- write clearer product descriptions
- confirm that weight and value are consistent
- retrieve invoice or certificate references without rushing
- avoid last-minute handwritten corrections

## Better than filling it in quickly at the counter

Completing the form by hand at the post office often means abbreviating too much, crossing things out, rewriting sections, or noticing a missing field only at the end. It is not just about convenience. A cleaner and more consistent form is also easier to review.

By preparing it first with **[the CN23.it generator](/)**, you can immediately see how the final PDF will look and print a clean copy ready to attach to the shipment.

## Practical workflow

1. Open the generator on your computer or phone.
2. Enter sender, recipient, and item details.
3. Review the PDF preview.
4. Print the form at home.
5. Go to the post office with the CN23 already filled out.

## Less rush, fewer mistakes

That is the real benefit: you remove pressure from the shipping moment. Instead of filling out the CN23 in a rush at the counter, you can complete it when you have the invoice, parcel contents, and recipient details in front of you.

If you want to save time on your next shipment, the simplest approach is to prepare the form in advance, print it, and arrive at the post office with everything ready.
